CREATURES

Cheetah

The cheetah is a large cat and the fastest land animal.

Eagle

Eagles are large, powerfully-built birds of prey, with heavy heads and beaks.

Tarsier

Tarsiers are haplorhine primates of the family Tarsiidae, which is, itself, the lone extant family within the infraorder Tarsiiformes.

Bear

Bears are carnivoran mammals of the family Ursidae. They are classified as caniforms, or doglike carnivorans.









Wolf

The wolf also known as the gray wolf or grey wolf, is a large canine native to Eurasia and North America.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land mammals on earth and have distinctly massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks.

Lion

Lion is said to be as King of the forest.It is a well-muscled cat with a long body, large head, and short legs.

Tiger

Tigers are powerful hunters with sharp teeth, strong jaws and agile bodies. They are the largest terrestrial mammal whose diet consists entirely of meat.
